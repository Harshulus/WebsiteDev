Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I reset my password or username?

    Click on forgot password and input the email you have used to register to the website. There will be an automatic website sent out with temporary password. Use that and change password by clicking on change password under user settings.

  • What will happen to my progress on dashboard if I am inactive for long period?

    All your progress will be saved and our learners can continue from where they left off last time. All your content will be auto saved as soon as the edit has been made.

  • What programming languages can I learn on Endeavour?

    Our learners can learn Python, Java, CSS, JS and most importanly HTML which is core for website development.

  • How do I report a mistake I have found?

    If it is a mistake in an exercise, you can just click the report a mistake button or directly contact one of the developers.

  • Can I change from English to any other language?

    Sorry, our website is only avaible in English. If you want translate the web page in other language you can use Google translator. We are currently working operating in different language. Stay tuned for future updated we will soon be operating in other languages as well.
